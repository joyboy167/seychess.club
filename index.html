Dev Log

Hi guys, it's Adam. Thanks for checking out the site. With your input and participation, I hope it continues to grow and foster competitiveness for chess in Seychelles. This space allows people to compare their live ratings from Chess.com and target different formats to beat their friends on the leaderboard. In the future, it will have more features like profiles, social aspects, reports on player strengths and weaknesses, and tutorials. We are also working to get the support of the chess federation to make it even more beneficial for everyone involved.

This project is built by the community. If you have any notes, suggestions, or friends who want to be featured on the site, feel free to message me here for further information.

This section provides information about upcoming chess federation tournaments and potential SeyChess online tournaments in the future.
We hope to find a format for players to compete for leaderboard head-to-head points and other competitive aspects. Watch this space for updates!
